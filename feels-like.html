No More Weather Guesswork

Most weather apps answer the wrong question. People don’t care about abstract conditions — they care about how stepping outside will feel and what to do about it.

Feels Like focuses on practical context and plain-language guidance so users can make better day-to-day calls in seconds.

Core promise: less forecast confusion, better daily decisions.
