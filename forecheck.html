Why Forecheck Exists

Most fantasy workflows are fragmented: one app for box scores, another for ownership, another for league chatter, plus spreadsheets to tie it together.

Forecheck gives managers one focused command center to discover streamers, track player direction, run custom scans, and make smarter adds with confidence.

Designed for: serious managers in active leagues who care about weekly edge and process quality.

Key Product Capabilities

Streamer Score (0–100) that actually means something
Forecheck ranks players with a position-aware score using recent production, deployment, peripherals, trend direction, and current availability. It’s built to surface practical pickup value, not just raw season totals.
Create + monitor custom scans
Managers can define scan rules (stat, comparator, threshold, window, and optional compare window), evaluate them instantly, then follow alert-enabled scans to catch new matches as they appear.
Player-by-player decision breakdowns
Every candidate can be inspected with rolling windows (L5/L10/L20/Season), trend context, and recent games so managers can validate the add before pulling the trigger.

Portfolio Build Notes

Forecheck is a native iOS product backed by a dedicated analytics service. The technical architecture is designed to keep the experience fast while supporting sophisticated scan logic.

Streamer Score model: Position-specific weighted scoring with trend bonuses and availability logic, capped to a 0–100 scale for quick comparison.
Scan engine: Rule-based evaluator supporting comparators, time windows, and differential comparisons (e.g., L5 vs Season) across stat families.
Alert loop: Alert-enabled scans are re-evaluated and notify users when new players enter match state.
