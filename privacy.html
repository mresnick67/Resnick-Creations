Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

Privacy by Design

This privacy policy describes how our weather applications, including "Feels Like," handle your information. We're based in Canada and follow privacy-by-design principles, collecting only what's necessary to provide you with accurate weather information.

What Information We Collect

Our weather apps are designed to be privacy-focused. Here's exactly what we collect:

Location Information (with your permission):

  • Your current location coordinates (latitude and longitude) to provide local weather
  • Location names you search for and save (stored only on your device)
  • We reverse-geocode coordinates to display readable location names (e.g., "Toronto, ON")

App Preferences (stored locally):

  • Your temperature unit preference (Celsius or Fahrenheit)
  • Your saved locations list
  • Weather data cache (to reduce data usage and provide faster loading)

Support Communications:

  • Information you provide when contacting us for support
  • Feedback you choose to share about the app

What We Don't Collect

We believe in transparency about what we don't collect:

  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No personal information beyond what's listed above
  • No automatic crash reporting (Apple may collect crash data, but we don't)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Fetch weather data for your current location and saved locations
  • Display weather information in your preferred temperature units
  • Cache weather data locally to improve app performance and reduce data usage
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Improve our apps based on feedback you voluntarily provide

How We Store Your Data

Your privacy is protected through local storage:

  • On Your Device: All your preferences and saved locations are stored locally using iOS's secure UserDefaults system
  • Weather Cache: Weather data is temporarily cached on your device and automatically expires
  • No Cloud Storage: We don't sync your data to any cloud services or our servers
  • No User Accounts: No registration, login, or user accounts required

Third-Party Services

Our apps use minimal third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo Weather API: We send only location coordinates (no personal information) to fetch weather data. Open-Meteo is a privacy-focused weather service that doesn't require API keys or track users.
  • Apple's Location Services: We use iOS's built-in location services with your explicit permission
  • iOS Sharing: When you share weather information, we use iOS's native sharing system

Your Privacy Rights in Canada

As a Canadian resident, you have specific rights under PIPEDA and provincial privacy laws:

  • Access: Request information about what personal data we have
  • Correction: Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Location Control: You can disable location services for our app at any time in iOS Settings
  • Data Portability: Since data is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary:

  • Location Data: Only used temporarily to fetch weather, not stored on our servers
  • Weather Cache: Automatically expires after 12 hours to ensure fresh data
  • Support Communications: Kept for up to 2 years to provide ongoing support
  • App Data: Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear the data

Children's Privacy

Our apps don't collect personal information from children. Parents in Canada can contact us if they believe their child has provided information to us, and we'll delete it promptly in accordance with PIPEDA requirements.

Changes to This Policy

  • App Store release notes for material changes
  • Updated effective date on this policy
  • Direct communication for significant changes affecting rights

Technical Details

For transparency, here are technical details about how our apps work:

  • Weather Data: We use Open-Meteo's free weather API, which doesn't require user registration or API keys
  • Location Accuracy: We only update location when accuracy is 100 meters or better
  • Cache Management: Weather data cache automatically expires and is cleaned up regularly
  • Network Usage: We minimize API calls through intelligent caching (typically updates every 3 hours)

Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • Local Storage: All personal data stays on your device using iOS's secure storage systems
  • No Central Database: We don't maintain a database of user information
  • Minimal Data Transmission: Only coordinates are sent to weather services, no personal identifiers
  • iOS Security: We rely on Apple's security framework for data protection
