Privacy Policy

Privacy by design.

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how our applications, including Feels Like, handle your information. We collect only what is necessary to provide app functionality.

What Information We Collect

Location Information (with your permission)

  • Current coordinates (latitude/longitude) for local weather
  • Location names you search for and save (stored on-device)
  • Reverse-geocoded place labels for readability

App Preferences (stored locally)

  • Temperature unit preference
  • Saved locations
  • Local weather cache for speed and reduced data usage

Support Communications

  • Information you share when contacting support
  • Optional product feedback

What We Don’t Collect

  • No usage analytics or ad tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No personal information beyond what is explicitly listed

How We Use Information

  • Fetch weather or app data required for product functionality
  • Render in your preferred display settings
  • Improve app performance with local caching
  • Respond to support requests

How We Store Data

  • Stored primarily on-device using iOS secure storage patterns
  • No user account requirement for core experience
  • No centralized user-profile database

Third-Party Services

  • Open-Meteo weather data APIs
  • Apple Location Services (permission-based)
  • Native iOS sharing features when user-initiated

Contact

If you have privacy questions, email resnickcreations@gmail.com.

As a Canadian-based developer, Resnick Creations is committed to respecting privacy rights under applicable legislation.
